The Beginning

Two people, different in ways that didn’t make sense together. Conversations that didn’t always align. Expectations that didn’t always meet. And still… they stayed. Not because it was easy, but because something quiet inside them refused to give up.

The Chaos

Fights came, sometimes over the smallest things, yet they carried the weight of unspoken fears and fragile hearts.But even in that quiet distance, something remained unbroken… and despite everything, they still chose to stay, to hold on, and to continue writing the same story together.

The Silence

Not every storm ends with words. Some fade into silence instead… heavy, slow, and thick with everything that was never said, yet deeply felt. But even then… through that quiet weight and unfinished sentences, they chose not to walk away. They stayed, holding onto something fragile yet real, letting their presence speak where words could not.

The Little Chaos

Then came a child. A little storm. A little drama. A little world that made everything louder, messier… and more beautiful. Silly. Annoying. Dramatic. But loved… completely, without conditions. this too

25+ Years

They stayed. Not because everything was perfect, or easy, or untouched by the chaos life kept sending their way… but because every time things fell apart, they found their way back to each other. Again and again, in quiet moments and in storms, in broken words and healing silences… they chose each other. Not once, but every single time.
